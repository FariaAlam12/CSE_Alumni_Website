Navigating my Career as a CSE graduate

Bonding is the most essential thing to grow as a brand. We are very glad that our seniors are making MIST CSE as a brand in different workplaces. We need to make our bonding more strong. Our juniors need the guidance of our seniors. We need a platform for this. As a result, the idea of the CSE Alumni Association of MIST (CAAM) came into our mind.We’re happy to announce that the newly formed CSE Alumni Association of MIST (CAAM) will be organizing its first seminar on “Navigating my Career as a CSE graduate”.We’re even more happy and excited to share with you the panel of speakers for this Webinar. We believe the valuable experiences of our beloved alumni, all distinguished and from such diverse work domains will definitely be a beacon of guidance for us!
Join us at 08:15 PM, Sat, July 18th as go live on the MIST Computer Club’s Facebook Page and chat with some of our respected alumni. Ask live questions to any of them and hopefully, you’ll gather valuable insights on the extensive career paths that a CSE grad might have! Don’t want to miss it? Fear not! Register here and we’ll send you calendar invites for the event and email reminders with all necessary info.
