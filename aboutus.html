The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) witnessed its first batch of graduates in the year 2004. Since then, a total of more than 1224 students have successfully graduated from our esteemed department. Our alumni have made significant contributions to various sectors, including industry, academia, and the Bangladesh Defense Forces. They have left their mark in renowned international companies such as IBM, CISCO, Oracle, and also in leading organizations within Bangladesh, such as Grameenphone, Robi, Banglalink, Bangladesh Bank, PDB, BTRC, and Enosis Solutions. Moreover, our alumni have excelled as faculty members in prestigious national and international universities, showcasing their expertise and knowledge in the field. Their achievements serve as a testament to the high-quality education and training provided by the Department of CSE.
